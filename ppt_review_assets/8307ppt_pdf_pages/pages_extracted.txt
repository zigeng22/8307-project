Why Mental Health LLMs Matter
Mental-health NLP requires multiple abilities:
•
01 - Recognition
Classify user mental health status
•
02 - Empathy
Generate supportive counseling responses
•
03 - Factual Grounding
Answer medical knowledge questions
General-purpose LLMs may:
- lack domain-specific clinical knowledge
- produce generic or weakly empathetic responses
- struggle with reliable factual grounding
We therefore evaluate whether LoRA fine-tuning and RAG
improve LLMs across different mental-health tasks.

Research Questions and Study Design
We evaluate 5 LLMs across 3 tasks and 4 configurations.
RQ1 — Baseline 
Comparison
How well do different LLMs 
perform on mental-health 
tasks without adaptation?
Zero-shot evaluation 
across 5 models.
RQ3 — RAG & 
Combination
Does RAG improve performance, 
and is Fine-tuned + RAG always 
the best?
Tests whether retrieval helps all 
tasks or only knowledge-
intensive QA.
RQ2 — LoRA Fine-
tuning
Does LoRA fine-tuning improve 
open-source LLMs on mental-
health tasks?
Applied to Llama, Qwen, and 
Gemma.
